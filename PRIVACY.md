# Privacy Policy for "Letterboxd Search" Extension

**Effective Date:** May 20, 2026

Thank you for using the "Letterboxd Search" browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe browsing experience. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
"Letterboxd Search" is designed with a strict privacy-first approach. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal or sensitive user data.** 

- **Browser Storage:** The extension uses your browser's built-in `chrome.storage.sync` API solely to save your specific extension preferences (e.g., whether to open links in a new tab, run searches in the background, or enable Google Search integration). This data is stored locally on your device and synced across your own signed-in browser instances. We do not have access to this data.
- **No Analytics or Tracking:** We do not embed any tracking codes, analytics services, or third-party monitoring tools. Your browsing history, search queries, and interactions with the extension remain entirely private and local to your machine.

## 2. Permissions Justification
To function properly, the extension requires certain permissions:
- **`contextMenus`**: Used to add the "Search on Letterboxd" option when you right-click selected text.
- **`storage`**: Used exclusively to save and retrieve your custom settings.
- **Host Permissions (`*://*.imdb.com/*`, `*://letterboxd.com/*`, `*://*.google.com/*`, etc.)**: Used solely to dynamically inject the Letterboxd/IMDb icons next to movie titles on these specific websites to enhance your browsing experience. The extension does not read or collect the content of these pages for any other purpose.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The extension creates hyperlinks that direct you to IMDb or Letterboxd. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. When you click these links, you are subject to the respective privacy policies of IMDb and Letterboxd.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the extension's GitHub repository or support page.
